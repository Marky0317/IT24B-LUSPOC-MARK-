Why We Started

Climate change is the defining challenge of our time. While the problem can feel overwhelming, we

believe that collective small steps can lead to significant impact. Founded by a passionate team of

environmental scientists, software developers, and community organizers, EcoImpact was created to

turn climate action into a daily habit.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has the tools to contribute to a sustainable future. By

leveraging technology and community power, we aim to inspire a new era of environmental

stewardship and resilience.

About us

At ClimateTrack, our mission is to empower individuals and communities with the tools and
knowledge they need to understand and act on the challenges of climate change.
Our web app combines real-time weather data with long-term climate trends to provide insights into
how the climate is changing in your area and around the globe. By bridging the gap between
scientific research and everyday experiences, we aim to make the impacts of climate change tangible
and actionable.
